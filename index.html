FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does GST registration take?
GST registration is typically completed within 3 to 7 working days from the date of document submission. We ensure all documents are submitted correctly the first time to avoid delays.
What documents are required for ITR filing?
For salaried individuals — Form 16, bank statements, investment proofs (80C, 80D), interest certificates. For businesses — books of accounts, GST returns, bank statements, TDS certificates. We guide you through exactly what is needed.
Can you handle income tax and GST notices?
Yes, absolutely. We provide expert representation and drafting of responses to departmental notices, scrutiny assessments and demands under both Income Tax and GST law. We have successfully handled hundreds of such notices.
Do you assist startups with company registration?
Yes — from choosing the right business structure (Pvt Ltd, LLP, Partnership, Proprietorship) to registration, GST, MSME, bank account opening and ongoing compliance. We love helping new ventures get started right.
What is the deadline for GST return filing?
GSTR-1 is due by 11th of the following month (monthly) or 13th of quarter end (quarterly). GSTR-3B is due by 20th of the following month. Annual GSTR-9 is due by 31st December. We proactively remind all clients of upcoming deadlines.
Do you offer online / remote consultation?
Yes, we serve clients across Surat and all over India through video calls, WhatsApp and email. Documents can be shared digitally and all services can be completed remotely with complete security and confidentiality.
How do I get started?
Simply call, WhatsApp or fill the contact form below. We will schedule a free initial consultation to understand your needs, explain the process and provide a clear quotation with no hidden charges.
